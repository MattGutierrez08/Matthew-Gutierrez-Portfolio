About Matthew

Matthew Gutierrez is a homeschooled student. When he was younger he would draw comic stories to make his own story be shown. When Matthew began drawing comics he began to write more of his stories and draw his characters. Matthew was very into the creeper from minecraft later naming his story "The Legend of Creeper King". He based his stories on his favorite video game Minecraft, then he began to actually write his "main" story in the year 2017. when Matthew was younger he would go to public school untill the end of 2018 where which is when him and his family moved out. Early 2018, Matthew began writing stories describing his character's life. Later in the year 2021, Matthew began to expand his story's universe. Then he began to add more personalized characters with better backstories and giving them a solid background. Matthew grew a very big interest in his own story making want to add more characters and more lore. Two years later in the year 2023, Matthew decided to put an end to his old and still current story "Legend of Creeper King" and starting from zero again with still some of the main characters from the past story. Matthew knew that the story he had made was not originally his, and if he ever wanted to publicate it it would be copyright. He started to make a new story with new characters that were not from other brands. Matthew had focused on more of an anime style for his new story. Matthew had come to a conclusion that he would finally call his new story called "The Academy" which was his original idea. He felt that this would be the perfect change of story that would be a good start. Matthew began to add new detail and backstory out his new story and mix some small examples with that of some current anime. After preparing everything for his new story he was ready to begin.
